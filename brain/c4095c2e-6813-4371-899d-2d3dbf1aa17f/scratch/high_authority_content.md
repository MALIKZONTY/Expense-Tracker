# 1. Privacy Policy Expansion (1200+ words)

## Introduction
Expensico Finance ("we," "our," or "us") is dedicated to the robust protection of user privacy and the transparent management of personal data. In an age of digital transformation, we recognize that financial data is among the most sensitive information an individual can share. This Privacy Policy is designed to provide you with a comprehensive understanding of how we collect, store, process, and protect your data across our platform, including our primary website (https://www.expensico.com/) and any associated subdomains or mobile interfaces.

Our commitment to privacy is not merely a legal requirement; it is a core pillar of our financial intelligence philosophy. We believe that you cannot have financial clarity without data security. This policy has been drafted to meet and exceed global regulatory standards, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in the European Union, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), and the UK Data Protection Act 2018.

## 1. Information We Collect
To provide a high-performance financial tracking experience, Expensico collects several categories of information:

### A. Personal Identification Information
When you register for an account, we collect your email address and, optionally, your name. This information serves as your unique identifier within our ecosystem and is used for authentication, account recovery, and critical security notifications.

### B. Financial Transactional Data
The core of our service involves the processing of financial records that you manually input or synchronize with the platform. This includes:
- Expense amounts and categories (e.g., Groceries, Rent, Entertainment).
- Income records and sources.
- Dates and timestamps associated with financial activities.
- Wallet or account labels (e.g., "Cash," "Main Bank").

This data is stored securely and is never shared with third-party advertisers in an identifiable format.

### C. Technical and Usage Data
Our servers automatically log certain technical information to ensure platform stability and security. This includes:
- IP addresses (anonymized where possible).
- Browser types and versions.
- Time zone settings and general location data.
- System performance logs and error reports.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
Expensico uses the collected data exclusively for high-value operational purposes:

- **Service Fulfillment**: To calculate your net balance, generate visual charts, and provide a historical record of your spending.
- **Security & Fraud Prevention**: Monitoring for unauthorized access attempts and ensuring the integrity of your financial vault.
- **Platform Optimization**: Analyzing anonymized usage patterns to improve the speed and responsiveness of our dashboard.
- **Communication**: Sending critical system updates, password reset links, and occasionally, high-value financial literacy insights from our blog.
- **Advertising Transparency**: Utilizing anonymized data to serve relevant, non-intrusive advertisements through the Google AdSense network, which supports our ability to offer the platform for free.

## 3. Data Storage and Infrastructure Integrity
Your data is stored using industry-leading infrastructure providers. We leverage encrypted databases (PostgreSQL) hosted on secure cloud environments (Railway and Neon) that implement physical and logical security measures. 

### Encryption at Rest and in Transit
All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is protected by Transport Layer Security (TLS/SSL) encryption. Once stored, sensitive account identifiers and financial records are encrypted at rest using Advanced Encryption Standard (AES-256) protocols.

## 4. Google AdSense and Third-Party Tracking
As an ad-supported platform, Expensico participates in the Google AdSense program. This involves the use of cookies and web beacons by Google to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other sites on the Internet. 

### Why We Use AdSense
Advertisements are the engine that allows Expensico to remain free for all users. By leveraging Google's advertising network, we can provide professional-grade financial tools without charging a subscription fee.

### Personalized Advertising (DART Cookies)
Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's [Ad Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads) or [www.aboutads.info](http://www.aboutads.info).

## 5. Your Rights Under Global Regulations (GDPR/CCPA)
Regardless of your geographical location, Expensico grants all users robust data rights:

- **The Right to Access**: You may request a copy of all personal data we hold about you at any time.
- **The Right to Rectification**: You have the right to correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
- **The Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")**: You may delete your account and all associated data through your dashboard settings. Once deleted, this data is purged from our active databases within 30 days.
- **The Right to Data Portability**: You may request your financial records in a structured, machine-readable format (e.g., CSV) for use with other services.
- **The Right to Object**: You may object to our processing of your data for specific purposes, such as marketing communications.

## 6. International Data Transfers
Expensico is a global platform. As such, your data may be transferred to, and processed in, countries outside of your own. We ensure that such transfers are governed by appropriate safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission, ensuring your data remains protected to the same high standards everywhere.

## 7. Children’s Privacy
Our services are designed for individuals who are at least 18 years of age (or the age of majority in their jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 13. If we discover that we have collected information from a child in violation of this policy, we will delete that information immediately.

## 8. Changes to This Policy
The financial and regulatory landscape is constantly evolving. As such, Expensico reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via your registered email address and through a prominent notice on our landing page. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about our data protection efforts.

# 4. Cookie Policy (1000+ words)

## What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used by website owners to make their websites work, or to work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information.

## How Expensico Uses Cookies
At Expensico, we use cookies for several critical reasons:
- **Authentication**: To keep you logged in as you navigate through the platform.
- **Preferences**: To remember your dashboard settings and UI preferences (e.g., Dark Mode settings).
- **Security**: To help identify and prevent security risks, such as unauthorized login attempts.
- **Analytics**: To understand how users interact with our features so we can optimize the experience.
- **Advertising**: To facilitate the Google AdSense program (see below).

## Third-Party Cookies: Google AdSense
We allow third-party service providers, primarily Google, to place cookies on your device to serve advertisements based on your prior visits to our website. 

### DoubleClick DART Cookies
Google uses the DART cookie to enable it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt-out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network Privacy Policy.

## Controlling Cookies
Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can set your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, please note that if you disable cookies, certain parts of the Expensico dashboard (such as authentication) will not function correctly.

# 5. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q: Is Expensico really free to use?
A: Yes! Our core mission is to provide high-quality financial intelligence to everyone. We support the platform through non-intrusive advertisements (Google AdSense), allowing us to offer our tracking and educational tools at zero cost to you.

### Q: How secure is my financial data?
A: Security is our top priority. We use AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS for data in transit. Your records are stored in high-security cloud environments, and we implement a "Soft-Delete" system to protect against accidental data loss.

### Q: Can I export my data?
A: Absolutely. We believe in data sovereignty. You can export your full transaction history in CSV format at any time from your settings panel.

### Q: Does Expensico connect directly to my bank?
A: To maintain the highest level of privacy and control, Expensico currently focuses on manual logging and synchronization. This ensures that you have full awareness of every transaction and that no third-party aggregator has access to your live bank feeds.

### Q: Is there a mobile app?
A: Expensico is a Progressive Web App (PWA). This means it is optimized for mobile browsers and can be "Added to Home Screen" on iOS and Android for a native app-like experience without the need for an app store download.
