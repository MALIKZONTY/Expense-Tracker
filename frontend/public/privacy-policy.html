Privacy Policy

Version 2.0 | Last Modified: April 20, 2026

At Expensico, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data stewardship. This policy satisfies requirements under GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and the UK Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Legal Bases for Processing

Under GDPR Article 6, we process data based on Contractual Necessity (to provide the tracking service), Legal Obligation (for tax and audit purposes), and Legitimate Interests (to improve platform security).

2. Data Collection & AdSense

We collect essential account data (email) and security logs. As an ad-supported platform, we use Google AdSense which uses cookies to serve ads based on your visits to our site and others on the internet.

Users may opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.
